Our Story

This summer, Brian and I moved across the country from DC to CA. We traveled with our clothes, books, and our 14 week old son in my belly. In Oakland, we live a very simple, cost-efficient, and healthy life and as we plan our future together (jobs, finding a permanent home, the start-up, the birth of our baby), we are making very intentional decisions about how spend our money and time.

In preparation for our baby's arrival, we are asking friends and family to donate to our homebirth fund instead of buying a gift. Especially since we have most of the big ticket items thanks to The Zaragoza and Wohlgemuth Families :]

Our decision to birth our son at home was made with the hope and faith that we can begin this journey in an empowering, affirming space without unnecessary interventions. A healthy woman having vaginal birth is the most natural thing that can take place on the face of the planet. I am so thankful that our baby and I are healthy enough to be candidates for a natural home birth. Ultimately, we have taken as many steps to ensure that we are mindfully prepared to receive this gift from God and the universe.

If you prefer to buy a gift, we do have a few items on our registry.

Team

Midwife


Shannon Staloch, Certified Professional Midwife (CPM) and Board Certified Lactation Consultant brings the most wholesome, positive energy and is extremely passionate about cultivating a natural, welcoming, safe environment for all three of us.

Midwifery care includes:
- Prenatal appointments every 4 weeks until 27 weeks, every two weeks until 36 weeks, and weekly until birth
- Care throughout labor and home birth
- Breastfeeding and lactation support
- Postpartum care in our home to ensure mother and baby wellness until 6 weeks after the baby is born
- Gentle and complete newborn examinations

Doula


Lenore Palumbo, Certified Birth and Postpartum Doula and Nutritional Therapist, is equipped with mindful comfort and massage techniques. We had instant chemistry with her specifically because of her expertise in nutrition and she speaks Spanish :)

Doula services include:
- 2 Prenatal appointments to discuss birth plan + comfort techniques + childbirth education
- Unlimited phone consultations throughout pregnancy and post-partum
- 24 hour on-call availability after 36 weeks
- Emotional support during early labor and throughout birth

Both offer holistic (nutrition, physical, emotional) 1 hour prenatal appointments in our home.

In case, you want to learn more about home births and doulas, we encourage you to visit Homemade Families. and Holistic Belly, respectively.
