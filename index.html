Welcome to our Birth Fund!

Together, Brian and I have lived a very simple, cost-efficient, and healthy life. We are very intentional about how we spend our money and time.

Our decision to birth our son at home was made with the hope and faith that we can begin this journey in an empowering, affirming space without unnecessary interventions. A healthy woman having vaginal birth is the most natural thing that can take place on the face of the planet. I am so thankful that our baby and I are healthy enough to be candidates for a natural home birth. Ultimately, we have taken all the steps to ensure that we are mindfully prepared to receive this gift from God and the universe.

Our Current Team

Midwife

After interviewing several midwives, we found it easy to pick Shannon Staloch, who is a Certified Professional Midwife (CPM) and Board Certified Lactation Consultant. She brings the most wholesome, positive energy and is extremely passionate about cultivating a natural, welcoming, safe environment for all three of us. In case, you want to learn more about home births, we encourage you to visit her website.

Doula

Lenore Palumbo, we found through a birth center. We had instant chemistry with her specifically because of her expertise in nutrition. On top of being a certified doula, she is a nutritional therapist and speaks Spanish :)
